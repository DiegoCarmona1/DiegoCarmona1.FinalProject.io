Basic Chicken Care

Hatching/Hatchling

Either by incubator or natural chicken hatching, or perhaps simply through tractor supply or other hatcheries, You have found yourself with new baby chicks. Now ytour task is to care for them as best as you can in order to raise them from mere hatchlings to full grown adults ready to take on the world and provide you whichever resource your raising them for. Whether it be for meat, eggs or simply for companionship, providing care during these necessary and vital time is required to fully bring the best out of your chickens. To begin there are some objects and supplies that may be necessary for you to obtain:

  • A large plastic tub in order to build a proper brooder for the chicks to stay in while their first weeks of life. A plastic tub is recommended instead of a regular cardboad box as they provide ease when it comes to cleaning the dirty, wet and messy conditions of the brooder.
